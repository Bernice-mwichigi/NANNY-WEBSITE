Frequently
Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our nanny services, caregiver qualifications, and how we support your family’s needs.

How do I hire a nanny through your service? +
You can fill out a request form on our website, and we’ll match you with a qualified nanny based on your family's needs and schedule.
Are all your nannies background checked? +
Yes, every nanny undergoes a comprehensive background check, including identity verification, criminal history, and reference screening.
Can I meet the nanny before hiring? +
Absolutely. We arrange interviews or trial sessions so you can meet the nanny and ensure they’re the right fit for your family.
Do you offer part-time or occasional babysitting? +
Yes, we provide full-time, part-time, and even last-minute babysitting options depending on your availability and location.
What qualifications do your nannies have? +
Our nannies typically have childcare certifications, CPR/First Aid training, and several years of experience working with children of various ages.
Is my family's information kept confidential? +
Yes, we prioritize your privacy and strictly protect all personal and family data shared with us.
